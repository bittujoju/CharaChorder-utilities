ChordFinder

This utility helps you identify new chords. It analyzes text you paste in (some text from you, such as emails export or chat export or other writing) and then shows you words in the text you haven't created chords for (based on the csv you give it). Note: your chord map never leaves your browser. Also, because processing is all done in your client performance could be heavily influenced by your computer. Long text may take a while to lemmatize.
