CharaChorder Chord Map Keyboard Layout Converter

This is primarily useful for users of the CharaChorder Lite who want to convert the standard QWERTY chords into Dvorak or Colemak chords.

Notes:

  • Everything is done client-side and your chord map never leaves your browser.
  • If there are chords in your chordmap with "w" present from QWERTY and you are converting to Dvorak your csv file will end up with extra commas that LaunchPad, as of creating this, does not currently handle.
