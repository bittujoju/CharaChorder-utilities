  • Count Bigrams
    • This tool helps you find two letter chords that are infrequently occuring in words so that you don't accidentally trigger chords.
  • Convert Chord Map
    • This tool helps you convert your chord map between QWERTY, Dvorak, and Colemak since the CharaChorder chords are based on position and not letter.
  • Chord Statistics
    • This tool looks at your uploaded chord map and provides some statistics (number of chords, breakdown by number of letters 2 letter/3 letter ... etc.)
  • ChordFinder
    • This tool looks at your uploaded chord map and a text and finds common words and phrases in the text that you don't have chords for.
