Here are some tools to help you with CharaChorder:

  • Count Bigrams - This tool helps you find two letter chords that are infrequently occuring in words so that you don't accidentally trigger chords.
  • Convert Chord Map - This tool helps you convert your chord map between QWERTY, Dvorak, and Colemak since the CharaChorder chords are based on position and not letter.
