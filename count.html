Calculate Bigram Frequency

On your CharaChorder device you may want to choose uncommon bigrams when using 2 letter chords to avoid accidentally triggering them when you are using chentry (character entry). You can use this form to analyze some text and find the count of all the bigram pairs (since the order doesn't matter when it comes to chording).

You can sort the table by clicking on the header.
